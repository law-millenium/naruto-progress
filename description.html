Replaces your IDE's progress bars with random shinobi.

If you like this plugin, please consider rating it on the Marketplace or starring it on Github!

Features

  • Over 19 shinobis can run for you.
  • Multiple colour schemes & styles
  • Replace loading spinners with spinning shurikens
  • Fully configurable: Preferences > Appearance & Behaviour > Naruto Progress
    • turn off & on shinobi sprites
    • turn on and off loading spinner shuriken
    • change color schemes & paint style
    • enable/disable specific shinobi or shinobis group (such as Konoha, Kumo or Akatsuki for now)
    • change speed of indeterminate progress bar

Getting started

You may have to disable some plugins that might be in conflicts with this one and to restart your IDE in order to make it work.

Here is the list known for now : Pokemon Progress, Mario Progress Bar, Sonic Progress Bar, Sonic Mania Progress Bar.
